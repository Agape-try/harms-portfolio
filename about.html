About Our Mission

We are a passionate team of developers, designers, and cultural enthusiasts dedicated to preserving and celebrating Ethiopian heritage through innovative technology solutions.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between traditional Ethiopian culture and modern technology, creating digital solutions that preserve, celebrate, and share our rich heritage with communities worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where Ethiopian culture thrives in the digital age, connecting diaspora communities and educating global audiences about the beauty and depth of Ethiopian traditions.

Our Values

Cultural authenticity, community collaboration, open-source development, and the belief that technology should serve humanity and preserve our collective heritage.

Our Story

Code for Ethiopian Culture was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that technology could be a bridge between generations, connecting young Ethiopians in the diaspora with their cultural roots while making Ethiopian heritage accessible to the world.

Founded by a group of Ethiopian developers and cultural enthusiasts, our organization emerged from the recognition that many traditional practices, languages, and stories were at risk of being lost in our increasingly digital world.

What started as weekend coding sessions has grown into a thriving community of volunteers who contribute their skills to create meaningful digital experiences that celebrate Ethiopian culture. Every project we undertake is driven by our commitment to authenticity and community impact.
